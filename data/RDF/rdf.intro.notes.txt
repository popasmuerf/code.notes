Introduction
======================================
The Resource Description Framework(RDF) is a standard(technically a W3C Recommendation)
for describing resources.  What is a resource ?  A resource is anything that we can 
identify:
	*	A person
	*	Location
	*	Documents
	*	etc

===========================================
